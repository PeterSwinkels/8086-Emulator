[Assembler]
-Only 8086 instructions are supported.
-A flat address target must be prefixed with an at sign ("@") and is only supported for control flow instruction.
-Single characters enclosed in quotes (""") may be used in place of a hexadecimal byte.
-Double characters enclosed in quotes (""") may be used in place of a hexadecimal word.
-Comments prefixed with a semi colon (";") are allowed.
-Enter an asterisk ("*") to overwrite the most recent instruction.
